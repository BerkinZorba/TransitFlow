About Us

TransitFlow aims to reduce the crowd waiting at bus stops by implementing optimized bus schedules. Unlike traditional methods where buses depart at fixed intervals, our AI-driven transportation scheduling tool analyzes crowd data at stops, considering factors such as weekdays, weekends, rush hours, and break times. Our innovation combines machine learning and computer vision to offer a complete transportation management solution, dynamically reacting to changing conditions in real-time.
